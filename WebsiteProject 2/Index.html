Is Astrology Prediction True?

+

Many believe astrologers can predict exact future events, but astrology is more about interpreting planetary movements and their influences. Planets shift between signs and houses, affecting their impact. For example, Mercury in a friendly sign generally brings positive outcomes, while its presence in an unfriendly sign may suggest challenges. Astrologers analyze these shifts to provide insights about upcoming periods. Accurate predictions are particularly useful for finding auspicious times and understanding personal traits
